    Description & Target audiance

    The bar and grouped bar charts are designed for senior managers to make decision on global energy sources production, however, the data in both graphs are presented in a way that everyone can get global and country specific energy source capacity production in megawatt. The data is grouped by energy source (fuel), country, capacity in meggawatt and depending on user filters data aggregation and tranformation is applied. The main reason why those area of information such country, fuel and capacity were selected is the availability of information for more than 95% of the selected columns in the dataset. Some of the columns in the dataset don't have data or are blank which makes comparison difficult or improper. However, reasons behind chosen bar graphs are visbility, readability and attraction. The data in both graphs are visible, easily readable and attract users by providing information, so users can get visible information in a single look. For more detail, click target audiance.
